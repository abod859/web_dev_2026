Welcome

About This Website

This website is a personal project created as part of an ICT course. The main goal of this site is to practice building structured and semantic HTML5 pages without using CSS.

The website consists of three main pages: Home, About Me, and Hobbies. Each page is designed to provide clear and organized information about me and my interests.

I hope you find this website informative and easy to navigate. Feel free to explore the different sections to learn more about me and my hobbies.

Who I Am

I am a student studying Information and Communication Technology (ICT). I am learning how to build websites using HTML5 while focusing on accessibility, usability, and clear content structure.

I am passionate about technology and enjoy exploring new tools and techniques in web development. This project is an opportunity for me to apply what I've learned and create a simple, yet effective personal website.

I look forward to sharing more about myself and my interests on this website, and I hope it serves as a useful example of how to create a well-structured HTML5 site.

What You Will Find Here

Use the navigation menu at the top to move between pages. The About Me page explains my background and studies, and the Hobbies page highlights activities I enjoy outside of class.
