Who I Am

My name is Abdullah Alhaideri, and I am a student majoring in Information and Communication Technology (ICT). I am interested in learning how web technologies work and how websites are structured using semantic HTML.

I enjoy learning by building small projects and improving them step by step. Writing code and creating websites is a great way for me to practice and apply what I learn in my courses.

My goal is to create pages that are clear and well-structured, and easy to navigate for users.
